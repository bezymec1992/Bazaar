About Bazaar

Bazaar – Antiques & Collectables

Founded in 1879, Bazaar has been a small but carefully curated antique shop located in Mullingar, Co. Westmeath, Ireland. Over generations, the collection has grown to include paintings, furniture, decorative objects, and rare collectables sourced from across Europe.

Each piece in our collection carries a unique history. We focus on objects that reflect craftsmanship, cultural heritage, and timeless design. From vintage furniture to small decorative treasures, every item is selected with attention to authenticity and character.

Our aim is to preserve and share objects that connect the past with the present, offering collectors and enthusiasts the opportunity to discover pieces with real historical value.
